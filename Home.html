Project Food

Locker

By: The Wall-ees

Front line workers have been proven to be at higher risk of exposure to COVID. Some of the most vulnerable are fast food or restaurant workers that deal with a higher number of people daily and are most often forget or not treated as a priority. Nearly a quarter of fast-food workers contracted COVID-19 in the last eighteen months, and less than half were notified by their employers after they had been exposed to COVID-19.

Project Overview

We decided to focus on this group of front line workers by creating the Food Locker which would achieve our goal of limiting the interaction of workers with customers. The Food Locker will feature motorized doors, displays, insulated inside, UV light cleaning, and smart autonomous features that limit the touching of surfaces to a minimum.

  • Our main goal is to limit the amount of physical interaction between employees and customers which is why we have created a touchless system that will eliminate physical interaction.
  • The prototype must be safe for all users. Multiple safety features will be integrated such as descriptive LCD screens with instructions, LED indicator lights, and UV cleaning lights.
  • Customer satisfaction is one of our main priorities which is why we integrated foam insulation to keep food hot and drinks cold.
